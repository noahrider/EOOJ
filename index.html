Democracy

Democracy is "Rule of the People". It is when the people (citizens) have say on what happens (voting on laws, presidents, ect.) But, did you know that Democracy was made in Greece? It's true! It's thought that Cleisthenes introduced demokratia (rule by the people) in 507 BC. This was the first known democracy in the world.


Geometry

Geometry. I know you hate it. Although it was made by someone in Greece. Euclid created Geometry around 6th century BCE and is known as "the father of geometry". He made it because the concerns of everyday life.


Olympics

The Olympics! Everyone loves these games that show the true skill of people. But, this was also made in Greece. Baron Pierre de Coubertin made the Olympics because he was inspired by the ancinent Olympic Games held in Olympia. So, if you like the Olympics, thank Olympia, Greece.
